Oppo Reno 7 Series Goes Official: Know the Complete Details

By Shivangi Agarwal  •  Updated On 25 Nov 2021
  • Like
  • Comment
  • Share

Oppo has finally refreshed their Reno lineup with the launch of the Oppo Reno 7 Series in China today. The Oppo Reno 7 Series has 3 models viz. Oppo Reno 7 5G, Oppo Reno 7 SE, and Oppo Reno 7 Pro 5G. The series is expected to arrive in India next year and before it does, let us have a look at the specs, pricing, storage, and color variants of the 3 smartphones.

Oppo Reno 7 5G Price & Color Variants

Oppo Reno 7 5G has been launched in 3 storage variants-

  • 8GB RAM+128GB storage- CNY 2699 (Rs. 31,500)
  • 8GB RAM+256GB storage- CNY 2999 (Rs. 35,500)
  • 12GB RAM+256GB storage- CNY 3299 (Rs. 38,500)

It comes in 3 color variants- Snow Gold, Starry Blue, and Starry Night Black.

Oppo Reno 7 5G Specs

Oppo Reno 7 vanilla variant sports a 6.43-inch Full HD+ OLED display with a 90Hz refresh rate and 180Hz touch sampling rate. It has a screen-to-body ratio of 91.7% and comes with Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection on top.

It features a 4500mAh battery with 60W fast charging support. The smartphone runs Android 11 based ColorOS and is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 778G chipset. In the camera department, it has a 64MP OV64B main camera, an 8MP wide-angle camera, and a 2MP macro camera. It also features a 32MP IMX709 selfie camera for video calls and selfies.

Oppo Reno 7 5G features a Z-axis linear motor, an in-display fingerprint sensor, and Bluetooth 5.2 for connectivity. It weighs 185 grams and is 7.59mm thick.

Oppo Reno 7 Pro 5G Price & Color Variants

The Pro variant comes in 2 storage options-

  • 8GB RAM+256GB storage- CNY 3699 (Rs. 43,100)
  • 12GB RAM+256GB storage- CNY 3999 (Rs. 46,650)

Oppo Reno 7 Pro will be available in Snow Gold, Starry Blue, and Starry Night Black colors.

Oppo Reno 7 Pro Specs

Oppo Reno 7 Pro is a top-end model in the series which comes with a 6.55-inch Full HD+ Super AMOLED display with a 90Hz refresh rate and 180Hz touch sampling rate. It has Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection on top.

The triple camera setup on the rear includes a 50MP Sony IMX766 sensor, an 8MP super-wide-angle camera, and a 2MP macro camera. There is also a 32MP IMX709 front camera for selfies.

Inside, it comes powered by an octa-core MediaTek Dimensity 1200Max chipset. The smartphone runs Android 11 based ColorOS 12.

It has a 4500mAh battery under the hood with 65W fast charging support. The smartphone weighs 180 grams and is 7.45mm thick.

Oppo Reno 7 SE Price & Color Variants

Oppo Reno 7 SE comes in 2 storage options-

  • 8GB RAM+128GB storage – CNY 2199 (Rs. 25,650)
  • 8GB RAM+256GB storage- CNY 2399 (Rs. 28,000)

Oppo Reno 7 SE will be available in Snow Gold, Starry Blue, and Starry Night Black colors.

Oppo Reno 7 SE Specs

Oppo Reno 7 SE features a 6.43-inch Full HD+ AMOLED display with a 90Hz refresh rate and a maximum touch sampling rate of 180Hz. It has a screen-to-body ratio of 90.8% and is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 5 on top.

It packs a 4500mAh battery with 33W fast charging. Oppo Reno 7 SE will run Android 11 based ColorOS 12 on top. It is powered by MediaTek Dimensity 900 SoC. On the back, it has a 48MP main camera and two 2MP auxiliary cameras for macro and portrait shots. It has a 16MP selfie camera on the front. It weighs 171 grams and is 7.45mm thick.

Additional products launched today

Oppo has also launched several other products alongside Reno 7 series today-

  • Oppo Enco Free2i Earbuds- CNY 399
  • Oppo Enco M32 neck mounted headphones- CNY 199
  • Oppo Smart TV R1 Lexiang Edition 55-inch – CNY 3999
  • Oppo Smart TV R1 Lexiang Edition 65-inch- CNY 4999
  • Oppo Monogram Printed Paula Black case- CNY 69
